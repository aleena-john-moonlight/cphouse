// 02. DATA RETENTION & PURGE CYCLE

We adhere to a Zero-Persistence Policy for non-static nodes. Metadata collected (MAC addresses and session timestamps) is utilized solely for DDoS mitigation and DHCP management.

  • Logs are automatically purged every 72 hours.
  • No Deep Packet Inspection (DPI) is performed on user payloads.
  • SSL/TLS traffic remains fully encrypted and opaque to the administrator.
